About

Crisis Text Line serves young people in any type of crisis, providing them access to free, 24/7, emotional support and information they need via the medium they already use and trust: text.

Since they organization was founded in 2012, I've been able to aid in an number of freelance projects. When they partnered with JCPenney as a featured JCP Cares charity, I created a motion video explaining CTL's purpose for both the campaign and broadcast television which helped them raise over $1,077,691. The video and supplemental marketing materials were featured on MTV Act and Self Magazine.
